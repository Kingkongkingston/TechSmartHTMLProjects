ROCK P-AI-PER SCISSORS

This is classic rock paper scissors, with a twist. Rock still beats scissors, paper still beats rock, and scissors still beats paper. The difference is that the computer will learn from your plays, and try to make educated guesses about what you will play next.

To play, click on the image which represents your choice: rock, paper, or scissors.

If you ever want to reset your stats, there's a reset button you can press in the bottom right corner of the screen.
